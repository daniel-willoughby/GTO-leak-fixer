Refining the Table

You picked No. 02 — Zen · Table. Here are three refinements built on that base: the lit felt as the hero, the cream Zen chrome, the muted four-colour deck. Same spot throughout — BTN vs BB, flop A♠ 8♣ 3♥, you hold A♦ Q♣ — three different ways to treat it.

2A

Table · Atelier

The same light table, perfected.

The original direction with the craft dialled up: a woven felt with a gold rail pinline, a soft table reflection, refined cards with an inner bevel, and a bet-size whisper (≈ 1.8 bb) under the call to action.

Faithful & premium
  • Felt weave texture + brass pinline + brass dealer button
  • Soft drop reflection lifting the table off the page
  • Keeps the exact layout you liked — just richer
2B

Table · Spotlight

A card-room at one in the morning.

A bold mood shift: dark, warm chrome with a spotlit felt and brass accents. The cards and the sage felt glow against the dark; everything else recedes. The most cinematic, immersive option.

Atmospheric & dramatic
  • Dark theme with vignette + a glowing table
  • Brass rail line, dealer button, mode + streak accents
  • Ghost “Check” vs a glowing sage “Bet”
2C

Table · Arena

In the game, with the controls to hand.

A layout rethink: a larger table fills the screen and a floating frosted dock holds the decision — including a bet-size selector (⅓ · ⅔ · pot). It feels like sitting at the table with a clean control surface.

Immersive & interactive
  • Bigger felt + glass decision dock (backdrop blur)
  • Bet-size chips with bb readouts — a sizing control
  • Minimal floating chrome to stay “in the table”
All three keep the felt, the cream chrome, Fraunces & Inter, and the four-colour deck of No. 02.
